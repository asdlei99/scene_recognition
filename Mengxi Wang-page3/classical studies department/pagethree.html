Mission statement:

Classics is in everything: it is myth studies, political science, art history, religion, psychology, linguistics, and medicine; it is great literature from epic to tragedy and comedy, history and satire, philosophy and the novel; it is even the powerful arts of persuasion and rhetoric. We explore them all. We look to the past to see the present. You can major or minor in Classical Civilization, Classics and Philosophy, Classics and Religion, Myth Studies, Ancient Greek, Latin, and/or Modern Greek.
